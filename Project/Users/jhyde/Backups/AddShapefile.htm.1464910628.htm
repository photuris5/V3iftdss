Add Shapefile

The FTEM Add Shapefile feature adds a shapefile to the map interface from your local computer. To add your shapefile, it must be compressed as a .zip file, and contain at least shp, .shx, .dbf, and .prj files.

Note that this will not save your shapefile, it is intended to be viewed while working with wildfires in treatments in the FTEM system. To permanently add wildfires and treatments to FTEM, please contact your Agency Lead, their contact information is displayed on the FTEM mainpage.

To add a shapefile from your local computer:

  1. Click the Add Shapefile widget.

  2. Click Upload File.

  3. Search your local computer file directory, select it, and click Open.

  4. Check any fields to include.

  5. Click Add to Map.

  6. Your shapefile isadded to the map and you are automatically zoomed to its extent.
