Using Landscape Summary to Determine Fuel Moistures

Landscape summary reports outline landscape features and predicted worst-case fire behavior (97th percentile fuel moisture and wind). To use this summary to find fuel moistures, follow the steps below:

Creating a Landscape Summary Viewing Fuel Moisture Values

From Modeling Playground, find your Auto97th model run and click on the name to show the options and click View Input.

A modal will appear displaying all the inputs that were run for the Auto97th summary. You can copy data from this box and paste it elsewhere, and click OK when you are ready to close it.

Need to know the RAWS station name? This is listed in the Auto97th summary report.
