Setting up an Account

To create a new account, select the Login option in the top right of the page, and select Register at the bottom of the Login box.

You will go to the New User Registration page, where you will input the required information and click the Register button.

Your User Name will be displayed on many exportable reports, so pick one you don't mind sharing!

Once your information has been submitted, you will receive an email from an administrator confirming your account has been created. You may log into at anytime after receiving this confirmation email.

When logging in for the first time, you may be prompted to accept the IT Security Rules of Behavior.
