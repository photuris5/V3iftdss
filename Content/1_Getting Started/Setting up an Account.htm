Setting up an Account

To create an account, click the 'Register' button at the bottom of the login dialogue box.

  • Enter your name and email and click 'submit' to receive an access code.
  • Once you have the access code create a user name and password and enter the access code to set up an account.
  • After setting up your account, you may log in.
  • When you log in for the first time you may create a user profile.
  • To start creating your profile, provide your first and last name and email address.  Your email address will only be used to send periodic IFTDSS-related updates, though other users will be able to see and use it to communicate with you. You can also upload a photograph or image to associate with your user profile. If you wish, fill in the other fields as well. Your name, agency, and location will appear in the list of users.
  • You can always edit this profile information later.
