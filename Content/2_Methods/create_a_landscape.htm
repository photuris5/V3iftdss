Creating a Landscape

You may establish your landscape by either downloading landscape data from within or by uploading your own landscape file.

Download LANDFIRE data to create a landscape from the
  1. From , scroll to the bottom and select 'Landscape Tools'.
  2. When the 'Landscape Tools' dialogue box appears, select the 'Create new' tab
  3. You may select your landscape by either choosing 'Select draw mode' to draw you area of interest on the map, or enter map coordinates for the North West and South East corners of your area of interest.
  4. From the dropdown menu, select your LANDFIRE version (LANDFIRE version 2012 is the default)
  5. Select the map resolution (30 square meters is the default)
  6. Give your landscape a descriptive name
  7. Select the folder in which to store your landscape
  8. When you are finished, select 'Create'.
