Landscape Summary

The Landscape Summary feature allows you to view your landscape in and generate a summary containing a table, bar chart, and pie chart for each of your landscape features.

To access the Landscape Summary from the wheel, click on 'Landscape Evaluation' which will bring up the 'Landscape Evaluation' table. Select 'Evaluate/Sumarize landscape data layers'.

This will take you to the 'Landscape Summary' to summarize an existing landscape, you may select it from the 'Your Landscapes' dropdown menu.
