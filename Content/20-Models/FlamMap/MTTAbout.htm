Minimum Travel Time (MTT)

Minimum Travel Time (MTT) is a two dimensional fire growth model. To run MTT from the Modeling Playground:

Click Playground in the top navigation bar.

Select MTT from the drop-down menu.

Select a landscape or create a new one using the Create Here button.

Ignition: Choose a shape, or a specific point from the drop-down menu

Wind: Select whether to implement Gridded Winds, Static Direction Winds, or Winds Blowing Uphill/Downhill. Next enter windspeed and wind direction

Spotting: Calculate the spotting probability
