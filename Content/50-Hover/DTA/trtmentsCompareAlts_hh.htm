Check the box next to two alternatives to compare them.

  • The first choice will be marked with a "1", that is your alternative
  • The second choice, marked with a "2", is your existing landscape

Tips

  • The Compare in Summary Report option does not become available until the landscapes and/or model outputs have finished processing.
  • Comparison reports are only available for 2 landscapes at a time. Selecting more than 2 landscapes at once will only proved a "View on Map" option, and only for the landscapes and outputs will be viewable in the Layer List.
  • If you want to compare the same two landscapes/outputs in reverse order you will need to delete the previously created Compare report in your workspace, reselect the landscapes in reverse order and create a new report.
