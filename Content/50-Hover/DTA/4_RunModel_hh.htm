Choose the landscapes to model fire behavior on. Each is assigned a default name, or you can type in your own descriptive name for each landscape.

Important! Once you select "Run Model" your treatment alternative session, inputs and resulting outputs are locked. Thus, develop treatment alternatives can only be run once per Originating landscape. In a future release of IFTDSS a "Modify Inputs" option will be available to facilitate calibration and make this process more convenient.

It may take several minutes for your model output to be created. Use the refresh icon here or in your Workspace for an updated status of model completion.
