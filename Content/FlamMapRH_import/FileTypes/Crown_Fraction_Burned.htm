Crown Fraction Burned

Crown Fraction Burned (CFB) is a fraction between 0 and 1 indicating the severity of a passive crown fire (Van Wagner 1989, 1993). A value of 0.0 indicates a surface fire while 1.0 represents an active crown fire. Intermediate values represent a scaled value of passive crown fire severity.

CFB is different for the two crown fire calculation methods available in FlamMap. A more complete discussion of those differences can be found in Scott (2006).
