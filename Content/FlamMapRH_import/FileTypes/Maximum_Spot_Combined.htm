Maximum Spot Combined

An output grid where the Max Spot Direction and Distance are combined into a single floating point value. Units for the maximum spot distance are in meters and the maximum spot direction is in radians. The intergral part of the floating point value represents the distance and the fractional part the direction in radians. For example, a value of 1351.54980 represents a maximum spot distance of 1351 meters and a maximum spot direction of 315.01 degrees.
