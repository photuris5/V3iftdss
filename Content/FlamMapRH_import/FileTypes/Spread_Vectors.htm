Spread Vectors

Vectors are a unique type of FlamMap output. A Spread Vector is a combination of the Maximum Spread Direction and Rate of Spread grids displayed as an arrow.

Spread Vectors are created using a basic FlamMap run and selecting the Spread Vectors check box on the Fire Behavior Outputs tab. Their display is controlled with the "Spread Vector Legend" dialog box. They can be exported from FlamMap as point shapefiles (with the Maximum Spread Direction and Rate of Spread as attributes) for use in other applications. The  attribute units for the export are degrees azimuth and meters/min respectively.
