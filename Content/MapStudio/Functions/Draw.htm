Draw

You may find yourself performing analysis, but not yet have spatial data available to upload as a shapefile. Within the , Draw gives you the capability to add:

  • Points
  • Lines
  • Polylines
  • Freehand Polylines
  • Triangles
  • Extents
  • Circles
  • Ellipses
  • Polygons
  • Freehand Polygons
  • Text

In addition, you can customize the symbology of your drawn layer to create custom maps.

After selecting Draw in the top right of , the draw box will appear with the various tools available to use. Use the available symbology options to tailor you drawn features to your liking.

Features added with Draw must be saved in a separate step, or they will be lost when you navigate away from .

In a future release, will have a Map Export Option. In the meantime you can use printscreen, or screen capture software to save an image of your map.
