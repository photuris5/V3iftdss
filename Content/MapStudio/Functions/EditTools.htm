Edit Tool

The Edit Tool allows you to create, edit, and save polygons (shapefiles) in , that can be used as areas of interest or landscape masks.

Save a polygon(s) you just uploaded to Draw a Polygon in with the extent, polygon, or freehand button and save.

Drag a Rectangle: To drag a rectangular extent, select the Extent button , navigate to the portion of the map where you intend to place the corner of the rectangle. Hold down the left mouse button, drag the cursor until you have your desired area highlighted in yellow, and release the left mouse button. To discard your polygon, use the Clear button. To edit your polygon, double left click anywhere within the area of the polygon. To save, give the polygon a name, specify the folder in which you would like to store the polygon, and select Create.

Snap Points: To snap a polygon using points, select the Polygon button , navigate to the portion of the map where you intend to place your polygon, and single left click the mouse to place your first point. Go the location where you want your second point, single left click again, and continue this process until you have drawn you polygon. Double left click to finish. To discard your polygon, use the Clear button. To edit your polygon, double left click anywhere within the area of the polygon. To save, give the polygon a name, specify the folder in which you would like to store the polygon, and select Create.

Draw Freehand: To draw a polygon freehand, select the Freehand button , navigate to the portion of the map where you intend to place your polygon, and hold down the left mouse key. While continuing to hold down the left mouse key, use the mouse to draw your polygon, and when you are finished, release the left mouse button. To discard your polygon, use the Clear button. To edit your polygon, double left click anywhere within the area of the polygon. To save, give the polygon a name, specify the folder in which you would like to store the polygon, and select Create.
