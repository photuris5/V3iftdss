Add Graphic

You may find yourself performing analysis, but not yet have spatial data available to upload as a shapefile. Within the , Add Graphics gives you the capability to add:

  • Points
  • Lines
  • Polylines
  • Freehand Polylines
  • Triangles
  • Extents
  • Circles
  • Ellipses
  • Polygons
  • Freehand Polygons
  • Text

In addition, you can customize the symbology of your drawn layer to create custom maps.

After selecting Add Graphics in the top right of , the Add Graphics box will appear with the various tools available to use. Use the available symbology options to tailor you drawn features to your liking.

Features added with Add Graphics may be printed with the map using your computer's Print Screen function, but they are not saved in , they disappear when you navigate away from .

In a future release, will have a Map Export Option. In the meantime you can use printscreen, or screen capture software to save an image of your map.
