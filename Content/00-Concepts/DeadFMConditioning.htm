Dead Fuel Moisture Conditioning

Fuel moisture conditioning is used to represent some of the variations in fuel moisture across your landscape. There are three choices for this:

Fixed

The fixed option applies the same fuel moisture (that you specified in the Fuel Moisture box) to the entire landscape. It does not account for variations i topography or shading.

IFTDSS Weather Stream

This option accounts for variation in topography, shading, and weather, across a period of time that you specify. The topography and shading values are inferred from your lcp file.
