Overview

There are four main components of the application. These can be accessed anytime, depending upon what you are looking to do, and all of them are available when you first log in. They include:

My Workspace

My Workspace: This is a list of all your files used in . These can be organized into folders. Clicking on the name of any file brings up options such as edit, delete, and view on map. When you first log in, My Workspace is available from 'Continue Working' . The files available in My Workspace include:

  • Landscape files (.lcp files) which is the basic file used throughout to represent your landscape.
  • Shapefiles that you upload and those that you create within
Modeling Playground

Modeling Playground: Here, you choose which models you'd like to run, and then begin modeling. All model outputs are also listed in My Modeling Playground. Clicking on a model output name brings up options such as edit, delete, view, Request a report (see results as tables and charts), or map (see results displayed on a map). You can get to this part of by clicking the Model Fire Behavior/Effects button from the log in page.

Map Studio: This is the mapping interface of , where you can create and edit landscape and shape files, view modeling outputs spatially, and compare various landscape features and modeling outputs across your landscape. This is available on navigation bar at the top of .

 The Fuels Planning Cycle

The Fuels Planning Cycle: The planning cycle walks you through the steps of landscape planning, such as landscape evaluation, strategic planning, implementation, and reporting. Each stage of the planning cycle walks you through the steps necessary to complete it. Each step takes you to the part of the application needed to complete it. The Planning Cycle is available from the Start Here button when you first log into .
