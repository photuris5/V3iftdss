Getting Help

You may access the Help Center anytime by clicking the Help icon in the top right of the menu bar.

Help Center - will take you to the Help Center main page and search bar. From here you can use the links, or search bar, to find full-length help pages, videos, tutorials, and technical documentation.

About this Page - will take you to the specific topic in Help Center that addresses the page you're using in , generally a step by step walkthough with video links.

As you use Help Center will notice Notes, Tips, and Important information

text marked "important" indicates information you need to know for the run or operation to successfully complete .

a note includes helpful information that applies to the task you are performing.

a tip includes information that will save time, or make subsequent processes in IFTDSS easier

text here describes features that will be added to in the near future

Q & A Forums - takes you to the Users forum where you can get help from other users.

Help Tour - Displays short tips and links within the interface.

There are also help embedded in the interface, either small question mark icons that display very brief tips and links when clicked, or help information displayed in the right-hand panel.
