Resetting Your Account Password

After three unsuccessful login attempts, your account will be locked. For this reason, its advisable to reset your password before using all three attempts in order to avoid having to contact Support to unlock an account.

Passwords may be reset once every 24 hours. Temporary passwords remain viable for 72 hours.

If you forget your password, reset it by clicking Forgot your Password within the login box at the top right of the home page:

  1. From the Login prompt, click Forgot your password.

  2. Enter your User ID and email address for your account, then click Reset Password.

  3. You will receive an email with a temporary password to use during the password reset process.

  4. Cut and past the temporary password into the login screen of .

  5. You will automatically be taken to the password reset screen where you will be prompted to enter your temporary password once more before creating your new password. Your password must be at least 12 characters long and consists of a combination of uppercase, lowercase, and numeric characters. When finished, click Reset Password.

    6. Passwords in are valid for 60 days before they need to be reset.

  6. A verification message confirms your password has been reset, similar to the one displayed below. You will automatically returned to the login page.

  7. You may now log into using your new password.
