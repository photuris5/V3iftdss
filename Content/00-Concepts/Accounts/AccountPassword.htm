Resetting Your Account Password

After three unsuccessful login attempts, your account will be locked. For this reason, its advisable to reset your password before using all three attempts in order to avoid having to contact support to unlock an account.

Passwords may be reset once every 24 hours.

Password reset codes are viable for 72 before expiring

If you forget your password, reset it by clicking Forgot your Password within the login box at the top right of the home page.

You may only reset your password

Next, enter your User ID and email address for your account, then click Reset Password.

You will receive an email with a temporary access code to login and reset your password, similar to the example pictured below.

Log into your account using the reset code in place of your password.

You will then be prompted to re-enter your reset code, and create a new password.

Your password must be at least 12 characters long and consists of a combination of uppercase, lowercase, and numeric characters. When finished, click Reset Password.

Passwords in are valid for 60 days before they need to be reset.

A verification message confirms your password has been reset, similar to the one displayed below.

Click Return to the IFTDSS Home Page.

You may now log into using your new password.
