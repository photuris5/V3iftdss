FTEM Account Setup

These instructions assume you already have an account. Once you are logged into your account, you may request an FTEM account. Having this FTEM account will allow you to access the FTEM database.

If you do not have an account yet, create one Request FTEM access for the first time

FTEM can be accessed from the Monitoring Stage of the Planning Cycle, or via the FTEM link at the top navigation

After selecting FTEM for the first time from the top navigation of , or from the Planning Cycle you are taken to the Request Access page.

  1. Select your agency from the drop-down menu.
  2. Select your Region from the drop-down menu.
  3. Provide a brief justification to help administrators approve your account.

  4. When finished, click the Request FTEM Access button.

You will receive a notice confirming your access request has been received. Once it has been approved, you will be notified via email and may then access the FTEM portion of .

This will allow you to access FTEM, but before you are able to use it, you must select your user role and get it approved.

 Get your user role approved to start using FTEM

To establish, or change, your user role in FTEM, follow the steps below. You need to do this to ensure you have editing permissions in FTEM:

  1. From the FTEM page, click your username in the top right of the screen and select Profile.

  2. Select the My Roles tab.

  3. From the My Roles tab, click the Add FTEM Roles button.

  4. In the box that pops up, select your desired role for your corresponding agency by clicking the check box next the role.

  5. Provide a brief justification for this role.

  6. When finished, click the Request FTEM Roles button.

You will receive a notice confirming your User Role request has been received. Once it has been approved, you are notified via email.

 Change your geographic area of interest in My Preferences

You may need to change your geographic area of interest if you monitor treatments in multiple forests or regions. To change the geographic area of interest:

  1. From the FTEM page, click your username in the top right of the screen and select Profile.

  2. Select the My Preferences tab.

  3. Click the Select New Map Extent button.

  4. Use the drop-down menu to select the desired Agency, Geographic Area, and Unit.

  5. Click Set default FTEM map extent to this Unit.
