Introduction to FTEM Roles and Permissions

Requesting a different role

All FTEM accounts are assigned the role of viewer by default. If you would like to request a different FTEM role, do so via your FTEM profile in the top right of the page by following the steps below:

  1. Go to the FTEM profile page. This can be done either by clicking on your username in the top right of the screen and selecting Profile from the drop-down, or using the Account Management button on the FTEM landing page.

  2. Select the Roles tab, this will display your current role, as well as an option to request additional roles.

  3. Click the Add FTEM Roles button, this opens the FTEM Roles table.

  4. The FTEM Roles table displays options to update your role for various agencies. Select the role you want.

    Viewers can only request the role of "Editor". Editors can then request the role of "Administrator".

  5. Provide a brief justification for your role using the justification text box. One sentence should suffice.

  6. Click Request FTEM Roles.

  7. A notice will appear confirming you have successfully requested an update to your FTEM role. Your request will be forwarded to an FTEM Administrator to review. Upon approval, you will receive confirmation of the new role via the email linked to your account. The next time you access FTEM you will have your new role.

 About Viewer Roles

Functions: Navigate the Wildfire and Treatment lists, view wildfires and treatments.

Who should have this role: Those who need to view wildfires or treatments but do not need to make edits or enter data.

What you will see: When you navigate to the FTEM interface, you will see the Wildfire and Treatment tabs on the right of the interface, as well as on the map to the left.

 About Editor Roles

Functions: Navigate Wildfire and Treatments tab, enter data on the Monitoring tab, submit data on the Complete tab.

Who should have this role: Those who report treatment interactions for their agency

What you will see: Wildfire tab, Treatments tab, Monitoring tab, Complete tab.

 About FTEM Administrator Roles

Functions: Navigate Wildfire and Treatments tab, enter data on the Monitoring tab, Submit data on the Complete tab.

Who should have this role: Those who need to review or delete monitoring data for their agency, those who need to manage other user accounts for their agency.

What you will see: Wildfire tab, Treatments tab, Monitoring tab, Complete tab, options to delete monitoring data, and a User list in your profile that will allow you to administer FTEM accounts.

Administrators will also see an Audit Log button for each fire listed in the Wildfire tab. This button generates a table showing all of the monitoring actions that have taken place for this fire in the FTEM system.

Administrator looking for help managing user roles? Check out the FTEM Administrator Topic.

FTEM Viewer vs. Agency Viewer? "Agency Viewer" roles are assigned to all account requests that come from personnel affiliated with agencies contributing data to FTEM (USFS, NPS, BLM, USFWS, and BIA). This status allows them to see FTEM Treatment status. "FTEM Viewer" encompasses all other users, such as collaborators whose organizations do not conduct FTEM monitoring.
