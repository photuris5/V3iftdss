Detailed FTEM Roles and Permissions

FTEM Role Basics

Viewers

Some users are pre-approved based on their agency email domain, these include the five federal land management agencies required to monitor fuel treatments using FTEM as listed in the table below. Pre-approved users automatically gain Agency Viewer access upon requesting FTEM access. All other users are approved by FTEM Administrators. Any user not affiliated with, or not requesting access for, one of the agencies below will receive FTEM Viewer access upon approve from an FTEM Administrator.

Agency Auto-approved email domain
Bureau of Indian Affairs @bia.gov
Bureau of Land Management @blm.gov
Fish and Wildlife Service @fws.gov
Forest Service @fs.fed.us
National Park Service @nps.gov

Editors

  • Editor roles, regardless of pre-approval, are only granted access after review from an administrator.
  • Upon requesting FTEM access, pre-approved users may request Editor role by checking the appropriate box upon requesting access. The box will not be visible to other users.

Administrators

  • FTEM Administrators will receive account request emails and are responsible for approving account requests for the following:

  • User requests for Agency Regions that do not have an assigned Agency Admin-regardless of email domain.

  • User requests for all non-core agencies (FTEM Viewers).

Changing Roles

A User’s role may be changed back to FTEM Viewer by an Administrator by de-selecting all checkboxes in the Agency Roles / Requests dialogue for a given user.

Pre-approved User Requests

Each dropdown below describes a detailed account request scenario for Per-approved users.

1. Pre-approved User requests a Viewer role for their agency (the agency that matches their email domain) and region, the edit box was not checked

In this scenario FTEM Agency Administrators will not receive a request. A Pre-approved user is automatically granted permission for the viewer role as follows:

  1. A Pre-approved user requests access for a core agency and region.

  2. The pre-approved user will automatically be emailed with an activation code to gain access to FTEM as an Agency Viewer.

 3. Pre-approved user requests Editor access for their agency and region (the edit box is checked)

  1. A Pre-approved user requests access for a core agency and region and has checked the edit box

  2. The pre-approved sser will automatically be emailed with an activation code to gain access to FTEM as an Agency Viewer.

  3. The FTEM Agency Administrator assigned to the Pre-approved User's Agency/Region will receive an email from the system notifying them that there is a pending Editor request for the user.

  4. The FTEM Agency Administrator accesses the FTEM user list and filters by All (users with pre-approved email domains are automatically Agency Viewers and are never Pending), Agency Viewer, Agency, and Region to find the user.

  5. The Agency Administrator clicks Grant FTEM Access under the user’s name to select and validate the role they are approving. In this case the Administrator checks the "Editor Role" box and clicks Grant Roles.

  6. The pre-approved user will receive an email informing them that their role has been changed to Editor. Upon login, the user sees they are now an Agency Editor in FTEM.

 

 5. Pre-approved user requests Viewer access for a non-core agency and region.

  1. A pre-approved user requests access for a non-core agency and region.

  2. The user’s r request is sent to all FTEM  Administrators via email.

  3. The FTEM Administrator determines whether to accept or deny the request. If accepted, the Administrator clicks the username to see actions available for that account.

  4. The FTEM Agency Administrator clicks Grant FTEM Access under the user’s name.

  5. The user receives a notification email informing them that their FTEM role has been modified. They may now log in to access FTEM.

 8. Pre-approved user requests viewer/editor access to an agency other than what their pre-approved email domain is (NPS email domain requesting access to BLM)

  1. A pre-approved user requests access for a core agency and region other than their own.

  2. The user’s request is sent to the assigned FTEM Agency Administrator(s) via email.

  3. The FTEM Agency Administrator accesses the FTEM user list and filters by Pending, Agency, and Region to find their user. Users with email domains that are not pre-approved will remain in the Pending list until they are granted Viewer privileges by an Administrator.

  4. The FTEM Agency Administrator clicks Grant FTEM Access under the user’s name.

  5. The FTEM Agency Administrator checks the box next to the requested role and clicks Grant Access in the dialogue box.

     

  6. The user receives a notification email informing them that their FTEM role has been approved. They may now log in and have access to the requested agency role.

    Users with more than one agency affiliation will see an agency box in the top right of their FTEM screen, and be able to toggle between the agencies they have roles for.


7. Agency Viewer with a non pre-approved email domain requests Editor role for their Agency

  1. A pre-approved user requests access for a core agency that is not affiliated with their email.

  2. The user’s Viewer request is sent to the assigned FTEM Agency Administrator(s) via email. If no FTEM Agency Administrator is assigned to the selected Agency/Region, the request will be sent to all FTEM Administrators via email.

  3. The FTEM Agency Administrator accesses the FTEM user list and filters by Pending, Agency, and Region to find the user.

  4. FTEM Agency Administrator clicks Grant FTEM Access under the user’s name and validates the role they are approving.

  5. FTEM Agency Administrator checks the Grant Editor role box and clicks Grant Access in the Grant Access dialogue. The user may now enter FTEM with their requested agency affiliation and role.

  6. User receives a notification email telling them that their FTEM permissions have been modified. They can now log in with their requested agency affiliation and role.

User Requests

Each dropdown below describes a detailed account request scenario users (not pre-approved).

2. Non pre-approved user requests Viewer access for a pre-approved agency, the edit box was not checked.

  1. A user requests access for a core agency and region.

  2. The user’s Viewer request is sent to the assigned FTEM Agency Administrator(s) via email. If no FTEM Agency Administrator is assigned to the selected Agency/Region, the request will be sent to all FTEM Administrators via email.

  3. The FTEM Agency Administrator accesses the FTEM user list and filters by Pending, Agency, and Region to find the user. Users with email domains that are not pre-approved will remain in the Pending list until they are granted (or denied) Viewer privileges by an Administrator.

  4. The Agency Administrator clicks Grant FTEM Access under the user’s name and validates the role they are approving in the dialogue box that appears by clicking Grant Access.

  5. The user can now enter FTEM as an Agency Viewer for their requested Agency and will be emailed a notification of their new role.

 4. Non pre-approved user requests Viewer access for a pre-approved agency, the edit box is checked.

  1. A user requests access for a core agency and region.

  2. The user’s Viewer request is sent to the assigned FTEM Agency Administrator(s) via email. If no FTEM Agency Administrator is assigned to the selected Agency/Region, the request will be sent to all FTEM Administrators via email.

  3. The FTEM Agency Administrator accesses the FTEM user list and filters by Pending, Agency, and Region to find their user. Users with email domains that are not pre-approved will remain in the Pending list until they are granted Viewer privileges by an Administrator.

  4. The FTEM Agency Administrator clicks Grant FTEM Access under the user’s name.

  5. The FTEM Agency Administrator checks the Grant editor role box and clicks Grant Access in the dialogue box.

     

  6. The user receives a notification email informing them that their FTEM role has been modified. They may now log in as Agency Editor.

 6. User requests Viewer access for a non-core agency and region

  1. A user requests access for a non core agency and region.

  2. FTEM Administrators are automatically notified of the request.

  3. An FTEM Administrator approves the request.

  4. The user receives an email confirming their access as an FTEM Viewer and may now access FTEM with that role.
