Add Shapefile

The FTEM Add Shapefile feature will add a shapefile to the map interface from your local computer. To add your shapefile, it must be compressed as a .zip file, and contain at least shp, .shx, .dbf, and .prj files.

Note that this will not save your shapefile, it is intended to be viewed while working with wildfires in treatments in the FTEM system. To permanently add wildfires and treatments to FTEM, please contact your Agency Lead, their contact information is displayed on the FTEM mainpage.

To add a shapefile from your local computer, follow the steps below:

  1. Click the Add Shapefile widget.

  2. Click the Upload File button.

  3. Search your local computer file directory, select it and click open.

  4. Check any fields you'd like to include.

  5. Click Add to Map.

  6. Your shapefile will then be added to the map and you will be automatically zoomed to its extent.
