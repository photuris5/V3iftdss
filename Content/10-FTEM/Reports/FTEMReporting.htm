FTEM Reporting

FTEM reports compile and summarize monitoring data for completed Wildfires for specific dates, regions and agencies. Report types include:

  • FTEM Wildfire Specific FTEM Report
  • FTEM Agency Summary
  • FTEM Region Summary
  • FTEM State Summary
  • Data Download

A "Completed" Wildfire is one in which all interactions for a given agency have been monitored with the required data, and have been submitted to the FTEM system using the Complete Wildfire Monitoring button in the Complete tab, located in the FTEM Monitoring interface.

When filtering by Year for reports, the Year is based on the Start Date of the Wildfire. This is important to remember for fires that may have begun in one calendar year, but interacted with a treatment in the next.

How to Generate a Report

Inputs needed to generate a report vary by the type of report. To generate a summary report:

  1. From the FTEM mainpage, click FTEM Reporting .

  2. Select your report type.
  3. Select the agency, if applicable.
  4. Select the state, if applicable.

  5. Select the year, or range of years.


  6. (For Wildfire Reports Only) Click Build Wildfire List and choose your wildfire
  7. Click Generate Report.

  8. Click Build Wildfire List to generate a list of available wildfires.
