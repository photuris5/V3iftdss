FTEM Wildfire Report

This summary is generated for a specific wildfire and includes a summary of all treatment interactions and the FTEM monitoring data reported for that treatment.

Report Contents

  • Map and metadata
  • FTEM monitoring data for each of the below categories:
    • Conditions When Wildfire Entered Treatment
    • Fire Effects Conditions When Wildfire Entered Treatment
    • Weather Conditions When Wildfire Entered Treatment
    • Fuel Conditions When Wildfire Entered Treatment
