FTEM Agency Administrators

The Agency Administrator acts as an official point of contact if there is a question about FTEM policy implementation not covered here in the Help Center, or if treatments or wildfires are missing and need to be added to the official systems of record for FTEM. Each of the five major federal land management agency has an individual to fill this role.
