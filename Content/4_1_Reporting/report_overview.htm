Data Reporting

Data can be sent to databases from within the user interface. Currently there are two databases linked to :

Monitoring Trends in Burn Severity (MTBS)

The MTBS is designed to consistently map the burn severtiy and perimeters of fires across all lands of teh United States from 1984 and beyond. For more information visit MTBS.gov

 Interagency Fuel Treatment Effectiveness Monitoring (FTEM)

The Interagency Fuel Treatment Effectiveness Monitoring database serves to:

  • Verifying that hazardous fuel treatments:
    • are encountered by wildfire on a regular basis
    • actually work when encountered by wildfire
  • Establish baseline data to assist with making future Hazardous Fuels Program decisions
  • Provide a minimum amount of information necessary to achieve a goal - not to overwhelm the user with information they may not need.
  • Ability to record additional information (optional)
    • Document lessons learned to adjust future treatment design and prescriptions
  • Archive for images, maps, narratives, detailed reports, and other relevant materials
