Swipe

During various analyses you may want to visually compare two landscapes, or compare model run outputs such as fire behavior with various landscape attributes.. This is where the Swipe tool comes in handy. The Swipe tool allows you to click and drag a bar across the screen to view layer interactions.

To use the tool, click Swipe in the top right of .

The Swipe tool box will appear in the upper right of the map, listing the visible layers in your Layer List, in the order they appear (Note that you can change the order of the layers in the Layer List by selecting the More Options button to the right of the layer and clicking the 'Move Up' or 'Move Down' options.

Select the layer want to swipe, this will be the layer that is hidden on the bottom half of the swipe bar as you move it across the map.

You may swipe between masks/polygons, landscapes, or model runs outputs.

In the tool dropdown the layer you select will be visible on the top part of the slider, the other layer will be visible on the bottom part of the sliders.

Layers and features must already be turned on either via the Layer List or Simulation Output Tools buttons before they are visible in the Swipe dropdown menu.
