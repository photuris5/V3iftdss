Identify

Identify is used to quickly reveal the information of any layer listed in the Layer List at any point in the . To use the identify button, select the Layer List and ensure the layer you wish to identify is checked.

Next click the Identify tool, you can identify map information by (a) point, (b) line, (c) polyline, (d) freehand polyline, (e) extent, (f) circle, (g) ellipse, (h) polygon, or (i) freehand polygon.

Use the dropdown menu to chose what layer you wish to identify from, or leave All Layers if you wish to observe information on all layers within your selected area.

To identify new information, click the Clear button.
