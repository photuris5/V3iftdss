Layer List

The Layer List widget shows all the layers currently displayed on the map.

Menu

The Layer List menu is located in the top right, it allows you to turn all layers on or off, and expand or collapse all layers.

 Turn layer visibility on or off

Use the check boxes to make different layers visible

 Look at individual attributes for each layer

Clicking to drop-down arrow to the left of each layer checkbox will display a list of the attributes within that layer.

Expand the legend for an attribute by clicking the drop-down arrow next to it.

Show or hide individual attributes by clicking the checkbox next to each one.

Zoom, move an attribute up or down, or adjust its transparency using the More Options button to the right of an attribute. Click the More Options button a second time to hide these options.

 Moving Attributes up or Down

You may change the order in which map attributes appear in using the More Options button to the right of the attribute and clicking either Move Up or Move Down. However, there are two limitations to this capability:

  • Shapes and shapefiles will always appear over raster data such as landscapes and fire behavior outputs.
  • Grouped layers can be moved up or down, but their attributes cannot. Grouped layers include landscape files and model output files. For Example, you may move a landscape, or a fire behavior output up or down in Layer List, but you may not move attributes within a group, such as moving the slope attribute above the elevation attribute within a landscape file.
