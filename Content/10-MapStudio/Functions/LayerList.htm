Layer List

The Layer List button shows all the layers currently displayed on the map.

Menu

The Layer List menu is located in the top right, it allows you to turn all layers on or off, and expand or collapse all layers.

 Turn layer visibility on or off

Use the check boxes to make different layers visible

 Look at individual attributes for each layer

Clicking the dropdown arrow to the left of each layer checkbox will display a list of the attributes within that layer.

  • Expand the legend for an attribute by clicking the dropdown arrow next to it.
  • Show or hide individual attributes by clicking the checkbox next to each one.
  • Zoom, move an attribute up or down, or adjust its transparency using the More Options button to the right of an attribute. Click the More Options button a second time to hide these options.
