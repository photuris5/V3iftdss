About the Planning Cycle

 

What is the Planning Cycle?

The Planning Cycle lets you navigate through to perform an ordered series of steps for different stages of landscape planning. Clicking on any stage of the Planning Cycle will bring up a series of clickable tasks associated with that stage. For example, by clicking the 'Landscape Evaluation' stage, you are presented with options to create landscape, evaluate and edit landscape features (such as fuel models) for accuracy, and run basic models to view fire behavior and prioritize areas on the landscape for treatment.

The stages of the Planning Cycle include:

Landscape Evaluation Strategic Planning Implementation Planning Monitoring Reporting
