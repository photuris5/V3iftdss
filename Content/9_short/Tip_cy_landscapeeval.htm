The planning cycle provides a navigation option to easily access operations common to fuels management and planning.

The Landscape Evaluation tasks include

  • Landscape Summary (acquire and summarize landscape layers)
  • Landscape Edits (edit layers)
  • Fuel Hazard Evaluation (Run fire behavior and evaluate the landscape)
  • Risk Assessment (Assess fire probability and landscape value changes)
  • Develop project objectives
