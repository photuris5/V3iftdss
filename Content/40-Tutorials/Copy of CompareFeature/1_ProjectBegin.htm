Beginning a Project

There is no formal definition of 'project' for , rather, the term refers to the collection of landscape files, model runs, and associated data for your specific area. Before beginning, create a folder for your project in My Workspace, to help keep track of your files as they are generated. To create a folder in My Workspace:

Give your folder a name, in this example 'Haypress Creek Project', and click Submit.
