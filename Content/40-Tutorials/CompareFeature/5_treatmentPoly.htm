Creating Treatment Polygons

To simulate and evaluate the impact of treatments, we'll delineate our planned treatment areas with shapefiles in . First we will create a polygon for each treatment area, next we will combine those into one shapefile so we can use the combined file in our comparison.

First, we'll create three individual shape files:

Open Create/Edit Shapes , select your method, in this case the freehand draw method, and draw your polygon.

You can use the delete button to remove the polygon if you are not satisfied with it. In this example, draw three polygons. After your first polygon is complete, select the freehand draw option and draw your second, then select the freehand draw tool again, and draw the third.

Note that your polygons do not have be an exact match with ones pictured above.

Your polygons will initially show up highlighted in yellow. You will want to save the three of these as one shapefile for later use in . To do this:

When completed, your polygons will change from highlighted to a purple shaded outline on the map as shown below.
