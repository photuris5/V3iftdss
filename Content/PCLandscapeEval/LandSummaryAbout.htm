Generate Landscapes and Create Summaries

The first step in working with landscapes in is to download a Landscape File (.lcp file), and evaluating the information.

Basic Functions

In the Landscape Generation and Summary Task you can:

  1. Specify an existing landscape for a summary
  2. Specify an area of interest (polygon or shapefile) within an existing landscape for a summary
  3. Summarize/Request a Report for a landscape or area of interest
  4. View a landscape or area of interest in
  5. Generate a new Landscape from within the Landscape Generation and Summary task
  6. Generate a new Landscape from within
To select and summarize an existing landscape
  1. Select your landscape from the My Landscapes dropdown menu
  2. Optional: Select an area of interest within your landscape from the Area of Interest dropdown menu
  3. Select Request a Report

When your summary is ready, you will receive a confirmation

You may view your summary from MyWorkspace by finding selecting summary files, indicated with a bar chart icon , and then selecting the View Summary button.

This will display a Summary report that lists out each component of the Landscape File on a miniature map, as well as in a table, bar chart, pie chart. Additionally, it will run a fire behavior report for the 97th percentile most severe fire weather and summarize fire behavior including flame length, spread rate, intensity, heat area, and crown fire state.

Visit the Auto97 Topic for more information on the Auto97 Summaries.
