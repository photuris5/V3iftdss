Treatment Rule Background

In you may choose to edit your landscape using your own custom rules, or default rules meant to replicate landscape treatments or disturbances. The default rules often have additional options that specify the severity or breadth of the treatment or disturbance. Below we have outlined the details describing the specific treatment or disturbance details associated with each default rule:

Thin: Slash Removed

Thin 20% of all species for all size classes: Pile burn - Understory thinning treatment that thins the stand to 80% of current density for all diameters. Thinned material is treated by pile burning 70% of the area and 80% of the fuel collected. Twenty percent additional mortality is expected in small diameter size classes due to thinning, piling, and burning. LANDFIRE vegetation disturbance codes 311, 312, and 313.

Thin 65% of all species for all size classes: Pile burn - Understory thinning treatment that thins the stand to 35% of current density for all diameters. Thinned material is treated by pile burning 70% of the area and 80% of the fuel collected. Twenty percent additional mortality is expected in small diameter size classes due to thinning, piling, and burning. LANDFIRE vegetation disturbance codes 321, 322, and 323.

 Thin: Slash Remains

Thin 55% of all material <6” DBH: lop and scatter - Understory thinning treatment that cuts 55% of all material < 6 inches in diameter and leaves it on site. Thinned material is treated by lopping and scattering. LANDFIRE vegetation disturbance codes 211, 212, and 213.

Thin 75% of all material <6” DBH; masticate ground fuels - Understory thinning treatment that cuts 75% of all material < 6 inches in diameter and leaves it on site. Thinned material is treated by mastication. LANDFIRE vegetation disturbance codes 221, 222, and 223.

Thin 90% of all material <8” DBH; masticate ground fuels - Understory thinning treatment that cuts 90% of all material < 8 inches in diameter and leaves it on site. Thinned material is treated by mastication. LANDFIRE vegetation disturbance codes 231, 232, and 233.

 Clear Cut

Clearcut and Broadcast Burn - Clear cut followed by broadcast burning across 100% of the area. LANDFIRE vegetation disturbance codes 331, 332, 333.

 Wildland Fire

Low severity fire - Low severity wildfire or prescribed burn in natural fuels. Forty-five percent of the area is burned under wet moisture conditions with a 5 mph east, north-east wind (70 degrees). Tree mortality ranges from 1-24%. LANDFIRE vegetation disturbance codes: 111,112,113.

Moderate severity fire - Moderate severity wildfire or prescribed burn in natural fuels. Seventy-five percent of the area is burned under dry moisture conditions with a 10 mph east, north-east wind (80 degrees). Tree mortality ranges from 24-75%. LANDFIRE vegetation disturbance codes: 121,122,123.

High severity fire - High severity wildfire. Ninety-five percent of the area is burned under very dry moisture conditions with a 20 mph east wind (90 degrees). Tree mortality ranges from 75-100%. LANDFIRE vegetation disturbance codes: 131,132,133.
