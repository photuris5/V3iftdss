About IFTDSS

Welcome to the Interagency Fuels Treatment Decision Support System (IFTDSS). IFTDSS is a web application that organizes previously existing and newly developed fire and fuels software applications to make fuels treatment planning and analysis more efficient and effective. has been developed for fuels treatment specialists by fuels treatment specialists.

Background Benefits

Because IFTDSS provides a single portal for access to several sources of data and models for fuels treatment planning, it conveys a number of benefits and solutions. Some of the highlights of IFTDSS are that it:

  • reduces or eliminates the amount of time a user needs to spend learning new interfaces and transforming data.
  • provides workflows that organize the business needs of fuels treatment planners.
  • makes it easy for planners to efficiently reuse and share their work products.
  • provides a consistent analysis framework for all users.
