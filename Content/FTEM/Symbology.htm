FTEM Maps and Symbols

The mapping features in FTEM consist of either points, or polygons. These are color coded to indicate the status of the treatment documentation in FTEM as either completed, in-progress, or not started. Similar symbology is also used when viewing FTEM data as a list. Below we list each of the symbols used in FTEM:
