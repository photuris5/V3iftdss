Modeling in Playground

The Modeling Playground is the area of in which models are populated and run; modeling outputs are also available here. The current capability of Modeling Playground is focused on fire behavior. It allows you to model various weather conditions and fuel moistures to ensure landscape fuel model parameters accurately generate expected fire behavior model outputs based on your knowledge and experience. This is also a place to experiment with “what if” scenarios.

As of this Spring 2017 Release of , modeling functions focus on fire behavior and risk assessment. Available models include:

  • FlamMap
  • Behave
  • Calkin Risk Assessment

The IFTDSS development team is working behind the scenes to build out the modeling capabilities. These functions will become available in a future releases.
