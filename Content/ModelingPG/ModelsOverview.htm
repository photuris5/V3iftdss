Modeling in Playground

The Modeling Playground is the area of in which models are populated and run, and modeling outputs are stored. You may select models to run, proceed to populating and running models, view a list of completed modeling runs, and select completed modeling runs to view output summaries or spatial outputs in .

As of this Spring 2017 Release of , modeling functions focus on fire behavior and risk assessment. Available models include:

  • FlamMap
  • Behave
  • Calkin Risk Assessment

The IFTDSS development team is working behind the scenes to build out the modeling capabilities. These functions will become available in a future releases.
