Meet IFTDSS

IFTDSS gives you the following:

  • One interface to run numerous fire fuels and effects models
  • Ability to download LANDFIRE data or use your own
  • View fire behavior and effects spatially
  • Conduct hazard and risk assessments
  • Create burn plans from an NWCG template
  • Share data and modeling results
