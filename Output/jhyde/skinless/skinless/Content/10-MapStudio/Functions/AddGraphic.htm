Add Graphic

You may find yourself performing analysis, but not yet have spatial data available to upload as a shapefile. Within the Map Studio, Add Graphics gives you the capability to add:

Points

Lines

Polylines

Freehand Polylines

Triangles

Extents

Circles

Ellipses

Polygons

Freehand Polygons

Text

You can customize the symbology of your drawn layer to create custom maps.

Features added with Add Graphics may be printed with the map using your computer's Print Screen function, but they are not saved in IFTDSS, they disappear when you navigate away from Map Studio.
