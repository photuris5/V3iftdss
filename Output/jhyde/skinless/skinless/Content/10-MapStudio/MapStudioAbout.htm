Map Studio tips and tricks

Other operations are closely tied to Map Studio, but may not be carried out there. These include:

  • Editing Landscape file: Initiated from the Landscape Evaluation stage of the Planning Cycle, using the Landscape Edit option. You may view Map Studio from within the editing interface, as well as view your newly edited landscape in Map Studio.
  • Generating Model Outputs: Initiated in My Modeling Playground. You may view your results in Map Studio

Currently Landscape files can be created in IFTDSS and edited.
