Beginning a Project

There is no formal definition of "project" for IFTDSS, rather, the term refers to the collection of landscape files, model runs, and associated data for your specific area. Before beginning, create a folder for your project in My Workspace, to help keep track of your files as they are generated. To create a folder in My Workspace:

Create a folder by clicking the Create Folder option at the top of the My Workspace page.

Give your folder a name, in this example "Haypress Creek Project", and click Submit.

Your new folder will now be visible in the Folders list.
