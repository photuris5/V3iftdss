The Planning Cycle - Strategic Planning

Strategic Planning takes users to the next level of analysis once the components and data within the initial landscape are understood and any necessary corrections to the Landscape data have been made.

Specific tasks include:

  • Develop Treatment Alternatives
  • Quantitative Risk Assessment
  • Prioritize Treatments
  • Develop Out Year Plans

Future releases of IFTDSS will add capability to assess risk, prioritize treatments, and develop year out plans
