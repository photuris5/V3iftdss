Notification styles

a note includes helpful information that applies to the task you are performing.

Referring to buttons and parts of

Specific buttons and Parts of the interface are given the span.UIComponent style

i.e. to upload a shapefile use the Shapefile Upload tool.

Sometimes we refer to parts of that aren't buttons, but parts of the application. These typically indicated by capitalization

i.e. To get started, go to the Landscape Evaluation stage of the Planning Cycle.
